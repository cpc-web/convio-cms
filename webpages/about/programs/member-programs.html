As a way of thanking members for their support, the Central Park Conservancy provides members with a wide variety of special perks, such as reduced ticket prices for official Central Park tours, special discounts at participating in-Park vendors, and exclusive members-only events.

To see a full listing of Conservancy programs, including those with member benefits, visit our events calendar.

Events

Central Park Conservancy hosts exclusive member programs throughout the year that offer members with the opportunity to learn and discover more about Central Park. Many of these programs are led by Conservancy staff and expert guides.

 Learn More

Walks

Water Walk: Belvedere to the Lake

A Central Park Conservancy staff led tour featuring some of the Park’s most prominent water bodies, including The Gill and the Lake. Route involves many hills and stairs. 75 minutes.

Date and Time: Saturday, August 8, 2015 - 10:00 am OR 1:00 pm
Location: Meet at Belvedere Castle

Pre-registration is required.

Please arrive 15 minutes in advance of the tour.

Hound Hikes

Hound Hike: North Woods

View the tumbling cascades and rustic bridges of the North Woods, a forest retreat located right in Manhattan, on this guided tour for you and your pooch. Take in the summer sunshine as you explore one of Central Park's most beloved treasures. Route involves many hills and stairs.

Date and Time: Sunday, July 12, 2015 - 11:00 am to 12:30 pm
Location: Meet at Charles A. Dana Discovery Center

Please arrive 15 minutes in advance of the tour. This is an on-leash event.

Pre-registration is required.

Classes

Drawing Class with Jessica Daryl Winer

Attendees will learn how to capture the beauty of the Park on paper in this drawing class, led by Centennial Trees of Central Park artist, Jessica Daryl Winer.

Date and Time: Saturday, August 1, 2015 - 10:30 am to 12:00 pm
Location: Meet at the Charles A. Dana Discovery Center

Attendees should bring a large sketchpad and pencil. No drawing experience is necessary.

Pre-registration is required. This is a members-only event.

Please arrive 15 minutes in advance for check-in.

Along with exclusive members-only events, members can take advantage of special benefits offered at public programs, including discounts on Park tours.

Official Central Park Tours

Learn more about the Park’s history, design, horticulture, and ecology with the Conservancy’s expert guides. Tours are offered year-round.

MEMBERS: Enjoy discounted tickets. For registration and more information visit centralparknyc.org/tours.

Birding Basics for Families

Experience Central Park when it becomes a precious bird habitat and migration hot spot! Bring your family and join Conservancy Discovery Guides and NYC Audubon Society on a guided walk: Birding Basics for Families. Learn how to spot and identify our feathered neighbors in their natural surroundings. Binoculars available to borrow, limited to 20 participants. Ages 5+.

Date and Time: Every Sunday, 10:00 am - 11:00 am
Location: Meet at The Charles A. Dana Discovery Center

Pre-registration is required for Members.

Out and About in the Park

In addition to discounts and pre-registration perks for tours and events, members are also presented with special benefits for regularly offered in-Park activities, perfect for the everyday Park patron.

 Learn More

Discovery Kits

Borrow a Woodland or Aquatic Discovery Kit equipped with binoculars, a hand lens, and flora/fauna guides for an in-Park adventure. An optional Discovery Journal with suggested activities and a large kid-friendly map will guide your exploration. Best for ages 6-10.

Date and Time: Daily, 10:00 am – 3:00 pm
Location: Meet at Belvedere Castle and Charles A. Dana Discovery Center

MEMBERS: Bring your membership card to receive a complimentary Discovery Journal.

Central Park Circuit

Reach your fitness goals in the world’s most beautiful outdoor gym – Central Park! Get fit with innovative exercises designed by top trainers to utilize the Park’s best natural features: rocks, trails, stairs, hills, and gorgeous open lawns. A variety of in-Park classes will help you to maximize your strength and agility, as well as increase flexibility and muscle tone. Free. Ages 18+. Classes are drop-in and open to all fitness levels. Space is limited for each class on a first come, first served basis.

Date andTime: Tuesdays and Thursdays, Summer 2015, 6:30 pm – 7:30 pm
Location: Meet at East Meadow

MEMBERS: Bring your membership card to each class for guaranteed entry.

Bloom Guide

Make the most of the season with the Central Park Conservancy Bloom Guide. Discover the horticultural beauty of Central Park with vivid photos and detailed descriptions of over 200 flowers, including where and when to find them blooming in the Park.
