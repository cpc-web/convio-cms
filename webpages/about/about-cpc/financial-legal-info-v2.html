Central Park Conservancy is responsible for the management, restoration and enhancement of Central Park and raises 75% of Central Park's annual operating expense budget from private donations. Since its founding in 1980, Central Park Conservancy has overseen the investment of approximately $750M into Central Park, $575M of which was privately funded.
