The season's most delicious event!

On Wednesday, June 11, 2014, 850 guests gathered on a beautiful summer evening for the annual Taste of Summer benefit held at Bethesda Terrace: a celebration of Central Park's beauty and New York's exquisite cuisine.  The evening featured tastings prepared by New York's top restaurants, a luxury-filled silent auction, and dancing to music by DJ Kiss.  Marcus Samuelsson hosted the Taste Cafe featuring dishes from Red Rooster Harlem, American Table Cafe and Bar at Lincoln Center, and Samuelsson at HP at the Fairmont Hamilton Princess Hotel in Bermuda.  35 additional restaurants participated in the annual kickoff to summer serving sample tastings throughout the night.
