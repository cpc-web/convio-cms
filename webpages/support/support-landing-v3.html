The opportunity to schedule a Day in the Dirt volunteer project is an exclusive benefit of the Corporate Partners Program. By joining the Corporate Partners program at the Pin Oak level, your team will have the unique chance to volunteer in one of New York’s most iconic landmarks.

Projects and group sizes are determined based on the Park’s needs, and may include painting benches, planting ground cover, or raking leaves. Your company will be acknowledged on signage displayed in the work area while activities are underway. If you would like to schedule a picnic after your volunteer experience, the Conservancy can provide vendor names and location suggestions.


SIGN UP TODAY

Please note: Days in the Dirt can be scheduled on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays. Each session lasts from 10 am to 1 pm and is appropriate for 20-25 team members. For specific questions about the Day in the Dirt program, please contact corporate@centralparknyc.org.
